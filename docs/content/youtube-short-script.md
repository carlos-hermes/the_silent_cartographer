# YouTube Short Script - The Silent Cartographer

**Title:** "I Built an AI That Reads My Books FOR Me"

**Duration:** < 60 seconds

---

## Script

**[0:00-0:05]** *Show Kindle with highlights*
> "I highlight everything. Then forget everything."

**[0:05-0:10]** *Show HTML export file*
> "Kindle lets you export highlights as HTML."

**[0:10-0:18]** *Show terminal running `tsc process`*
> "So I built a tool that processes them with AI."

**[0:18-0:30]** *Show color explanation graphic*
> "Yellow highlights become concept notes.
> Pink becomes Asana tasks.
> Blue preserves quotes.
> Orange captures disagreements."

**[0:30-0:40]** *Show Obsidian note with philosophical framework*
> "Each concept gets analyzed through 9 philosophical frameworks. Dialectics. Socratic questioning. Scientific method."

**[0:40-0:48]** *Show spaced repetition reminder*
> "Then it reminds me to review at spaced intervals."

**[0:48-0:53]** *Show dashboard with stats*
> "50 books. 400 concepts. Actually remembered."

**[0:53-0:60]** *Back to face*
> "The gap between reading and knowing? It's a system."
